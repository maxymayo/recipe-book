# Chicken salad

## Ingredients

- 400g Chicken
- 2 Tbsp Mayonnaise
- 0.5 Leek
- Some water



## Instructions

- Buy the ingredients
- Forget that you have them in your fridge
- Let a few days pass in blissful ignorance
- Go through your fridge one day and remember that you wanted to make it
- Toss the ingredients because they are past last use date
- Repeat
